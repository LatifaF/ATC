Giraffe

A giraffe's neck is too short to reach the ground. it has to spread its front legs to reach the ground for a drink of water.

  • Average Height: Between 16 and 20 feet.
  • Average Speed: 35 miles an hour
  • Average Lifespan: 25 years
  • Habitat: natives of Africa,run wild in the tall grasses of the open planes.

Giraffes are known as the tallest mammals in the world and reach heights of over 16 feet. Their necks are the tallest portions of their bodies and are used by the males to fight with other males for mates.
